## Video Template: Destination Guide

### Script Structure

```
[HOOK - 0:00-0:15]
"Sigiriya Rock Fortress - one of Asia's most incredible ancient sites. 
In this guide, I'll show you everything you need to know before visiting."

[INTRO - 0:15-0:45]
- Welcome to Recharge Travels
- What we'll cover in this video
- Ask viewers to subscribe

[OVERVIEW - 0:45-2:00]
- What is Sigiriya?
- Quick history (30 seconds)
- Why it's important

[PRACTICAL INFO - 2:00-5:00]
- Entry fee: $30 USD
- Opening hours: 7am-5:30pm
- How long to allow: 2-3 hours
- Best time to visit: Early morning

[THE EXPERIENCE - 5:00-10:00]
- What to expect (walkthrough)
- Key highlights
- Photo opportunities
- Tips from our guides

[HOW TO GET THERE - 10:00-11:00]
- From Colombo
- From Kandy
- Tour options

[FAQ - 11:00-12:00]
- Answer 2-3 common questions
- "Is Sigiriya worth it?"

[CALL TO ACTION - 12:00-end]
- Subscribe reminder
- Link to book a tour
- Next video suggestion
```

### Thumbnail Formula

**Elements:**
1. **High-quality destination image** (bright, saturated)
2. **2-4 words of text** (large, readable)
3. **Your face or icon** (builds recognition)
4. **Contrasting colors** (stand out in feed)
5. **Arrow or indicator** (draws attention)

**Example Thumbnails:**
- Sigiriya: Person pointing at rock + "ULTIMATE GUIDE"
- Safari: Leopard close-up + "I SAW THIS!"
- Train: Train on bridge + "BEST TRAIN EVER"
